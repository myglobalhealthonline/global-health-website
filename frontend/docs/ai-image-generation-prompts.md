# AI Image Generation Prompts

Style rules for all prompts:
- clean healthcare platform
- calm and professional
- soft green overlays
- rounded shapes
- subtle lime-green blobs
- modern medical illustrations
- friendly but trustworthy
- no clutter
- bright white background
- no embedded text
- no logos unless explicitly requested for the temporary wordmark concept
- no emergency scenes, surgery, needles, blood, medication brands, or real hospital logos
